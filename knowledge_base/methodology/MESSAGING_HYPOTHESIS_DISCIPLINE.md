# Messaging Hypothesis Discipline

[VERIFIED: Josh Pappas call 2026-04-06]

All messaging is a **hypothesis** until validated by outreach data. This is not a philosophical position - it's an operational discipline.

## The Principle

> "The overall approach is to treat messaging as a hypothesis that will be supported or disproved by data from reaching out to potential clients."
> - Josh Pappas, 2026-04-06

## Operational Implications

### What This Means

1. **No messaging is "done"** - every sequence is a test
2. **Track response data** - opens, replies, meeting conversions
3. **Iterate rapidly** - change what doesn't work within days, not weeks
4. **Document learnings** - every learning is critical for defining the framework

### What This Does NOT Mean

- It does not mean messaging is random or unstructured
- It does not mean we skip research before outreach
- It does not mean we ignore best practices

## Current Messaging Hypothesis

**Tier 1A/1B (Mid-market providers, PCP groups):**
- Lead with operational owner, not clinical champion
- Frame mental health identification as ROI issue
- Tie to reimbursement codes (CoCM, BHI CPT codes)
- Use permissionless value propositions from public data

**Validation Criteria:**
- 5 office managers on phone in 30 days ([[THIRTY_DAY_OFFICE_MANAGER_TARGET]])
- Track which angle generates meetings vs. which generates silence
