# CoCM Outreach Sequencing

The deployment guide converts grades and org types into execution rules. The core point is that outbound order should change by evidence strength, not just ICP fit.

## Tier rules

- Tier 1: clinical leader first, finance second, informatics third.
- Tier 2: clinical leader only at first; numerical range stays out of written outbound if the grade is weak.
- Tier 3: nurture only; category story, not account-specific range.
- Tier 4: do not outbound on public-signal logic.

## Numerical-claims rule

- Grades `A/B`: modeled range may appear with caveats.
- Grade `C`: do not lead written outbound with specific modeled numbers.
- Grade `D`: do not use modeled numbers for outbound.

## Why this matters

This sequencing turns the estimate engine into a governed GTM wedge instead of a spreadsheet that reps can overuse.
