# 30-Day Target: 5 Office Managers on Phone

[VERIFIED: Josh Pappas call 2026-04-06]

The first concrete milestone for outreach is **5 office manager conversations within 30 days**. This is not arbitrary - it's the minimum viable signal that messaging resonates.

## Why Office Managers

Office managers and practice administrators are the **operational owners** in Tier 1A/1B accounts:

| Role | Why Target |
|------|------------|
| Office Manager | Daily workflow pain, budget influence |
| Practice Administrator | Operational decisions, vendor relationships |
| Clinical Manager | Bridge to clinical champion |

They feel the problem daily and can champion internally. Going to CMO/CFO cold without internal champion creates price-only conversations.

## The Math

**Assumption:** Email warm-up requires time, so early outreach is constrained.

**Target Funnel (30 days):**
- 500-1000 emails sent (depending on domain/inbox warm-up)
- 10-20% open rate = 50-200 opens
- 2-5% reply rate = 10-50 replies
- 10-20% meeting conversion = 5-10 calls

**5 calls is the floor** - fewer suggests messaging miss or targeting miss.

## What Success Looks Like

A successful 30-day period delivers:

1. **5+ live conversations** with office managers/practice admins
2. **Documented objections** - what do they push back on?
3. **Validated pain points** - does mental health workload resonate?
4. **Pipeline seeds** - any that could become pilots?

## What Failure Looks Like

If 30 days pass without 5 calls:

1. **Messaging hypothesis failed** - revisit angle and value prop
2. **Targeting hypothesis failed** - revisit ICP and list criteria
3. **Channel hypothesis failed** - email may not be right channel

Document the failure and iterate. Do not keep sending the same message expecting different results.
