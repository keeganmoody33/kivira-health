# Evidence discipline (research synthesis)

Use a **three-layer evidence model** in GTM:

1. **Public marketing claims** (website, decks) — highest scrutiny; align with legal/clinical review.
2. **Established instruments** (PHQ-9, GAD-7, etc.) — cite primary literature via [[CLINICAL_INSTRUMENTS_SURFACE]]; do not imply those papers validate Kivira’s product performance.
3. **Kivira-specific validation** — treat as **gap** until transparent studies exist ([[COMPETITIVE_EVIDENCE_GAP]]).

## Key Points

- When buyers ask “what’s your accuracy?”, route to **approved** figures or to **study roadmap**—never to animated marketing charts alone.
- Partner with product/clinical to maintain an **evidence roadmap** slide with dates and study types.
