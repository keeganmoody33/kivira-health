# Kivira Engagement Structure — JP Intermediary Model

The Kivira GTM work in this repo exists under a **contracted-intermediary** arrangement, not a direct Kivira employment or vendor relationship. **Josh Pappas (JP)** is a contracted GTM operator/advisor who holds the relationship with Kivira's founder and **subcontracts the outbound execution to Keegan**. JP takes the advisory and deal/closing layer "over top"; Keegan owns list-building, messaging, sequencing, and the outbound infrastructure. This is why ~18 existing nodes cite JP as their `source` — he is the conduit through which Kivira direction reaches this graph, not a Kivira employee.

The engagement opened **2026-03-25** as a 3-month minimum pilot, framed explicitly as *prove value first, then expand*: JP's stated arc is to demonstrate results over the minimum term and then bring Keegan on full-time day-to-day while JP stays on advisory/closing. The repeated discipline from JP — and the reason it matters for scope decisions — is **execution over engineering**: book demos and gather message learnings inside a capped weekly time box rather than over-build the framework.

The Kivira-side stakeholder is **Maria T Carmona, MBA (Founder & CEO — see [[KIVIRA_COMPANY_PROFILE_2026_05_11]])**. In *this engagement* she is the proposal recipient, the payment gate (work funds flow after she pays JP), runs the board-feedback loop on the proposal, and personally attends/qualifies demos. A second Kivira contact, **Chris**, appears in demo coordination. Buyer-side email domain is `@kivira.health` (e.g. `mariat@kivira.health`).

## Key Points

- **Model:** JP = contracted intermediary/advisor; Keegan = subcontracted outbound execution. Not direct Kivira employment.
- **Comp (structure, not plumbing):** ~$2,000/month retainer to Keegan **+ ~$200 per booked meeting**, against **~10 hours/month (≈2 hrs/week) of dedicated execution time**. 3-month minimum so funds can be taken up front.
- **Time-box discipline:** the cap is real — JP repeatedly reminds "don't over-burn," ~2 focused hours/week, mostly calls/emails to the live list.
- **Day-60 goal:** **4 booked demos** (1 already booked as of 2026-05-13).
- **Reporting cadence (JP directive):** weekly updates + metrics due **Thursday night** — emails/calls counts, feedback, screenshots; "demos will come, the data is what we need now."
- **Channel evolution:** built outbound infra (domains/inboxes, 30-day warmup) early, then by mid-May JP redirected toward a **manual ACO blitz** — running lists + manual calls/emails to a targeted ACO list (origin of [[ACO_ATTACK_MOTION_2A_PRIMARY]]).
- **Scope guardrail JP set:** deliver execution and a *framework for scale* as the value-add, not the full proprietary playbook ("don't give them the whole value").
