# GTM 30-60-90 Execution Cadence

The execution framework for Kivira's go-to-market motion, structured in 30-day phases with specific objectives and success criteria.

[INFERRED: Gemini directional analysis - verify before external use]

## Day 1-30: Build / Test / Warm Up

**Objective:** Establish foundation and begin outreach

**Activities:**
- Build out targeting lists (Provider/PCP segment)
- Set up outreach infrastructure (email sequences, call scripts)
- Warm up domains/accounts
- Begin dialing office managers and practice admins to secure meetings
- Refine messaging based on initial responses
- Deliver tech stack document (domains, inboxes, warm-up math)

**Success Criteria:**
- **5 office managers on the phone** ([[THIRTY_DAY_OFFICE_MANAGER_TARGET]]) - this is the minimum viable signal
- Outreach infrastructure operational
- Initial response rate baseline established
- Documented objections from live conversations

**Key Insight:** Target operational owners first (office managers, practice admins), not clinical champions. They feel the problem daily and can champion internally.

## Day 30-60: Measure Results, Signals

**Objective:** Validate messaging and identify patterns

**Activities:**
- Analyze meeting conversion rates
- Document objections and responses
- Identify high-converting segments/personas
- Refine targeting based on signal quality
- Begin pilot conversations with interested prospects

**Success Criteria:**
- Clear understanding of what resonates
- Objection handling playbook started
- Pipeline of pilot candidates identified

## Day 90+: Results, Optimize

**Objective:** Scale what works

**Activities:**
- Double down on high-signal segments
- Launch pilots with qualified prospects
- Build case studies from early wins
- Begin expansion to Segment 2 (Health Systems)
- Optimize based on data

**Success Criteria:**
- Active pilots in market
- Repeatable sales motion documented
- Foundation for Segment 2 expansion

## Key Principles

1. **Focus on Segment 1 first** - Don't chase enterprise deals before proving the model
2. **Signal > Volume** - Quality of meetings matters more than quantity
3. **Build the playbook** - Every call improves the methodology
4. **Messaging is hypothesis** - Treat all messaging as hypothesis to be validated by outreach data ([[MESSAGING_HYPOTHESIS_DISCIPLINE]])
5. **Pilots before scale** - Secure pilot sites to prove value before investing in scaled GTM ([[PILOT_SITE_ACQUISITION_PRIORITY]])
