# Outreach Wave Structure

[VERIFIED: Based on CMS-validated TAM with contact volume calculations]

Structured wave approach to outreach execution across the 23,705 contact universe.

## Wave Overview

| Wave | Subtiers | Contacts | Timing | Why This Order |
|------|----------|----------|--------|----------------|
| Wave 1 | 1C (high-fit) + 2A (high-fit) | ~2,500 | Weeks 1-4 | Highest pain, CMS contact data, VBC urgency |
| Wave 2 | 1A (10-30 PCPs) + 1C (remaining) | ~5,000 | Weeks 3-8 | Core mid-market, proven messaging |
| Wave 3 | 1B + 2C + 1A (5-9 PCPs) | ~8,000 | Weeks 6-12 | Broader reach, refined from learnings |
| Parallel | 3A + 3B + 3C | ~1,740 | Ongoing | Enterprise cadence, lower volume |
| Targeted | 2B | ~200 | Ongoing | Partnership conversations, not campaigns |

## Wave 1: High-Fit VBC + ACOs (~2,500 contacts)

**Subtiers:** 1C (high-fit) + 2A (high-fit)

**Why First:**
- Highest pain point (CMS V28 urgency)
- Best data quality
- 2A has FREE contact data from CMS (exec name, email, phone)
- VBC groups have CoCM readiness

**Criteria:**
- `pilot_feasibility_score ≥ 7`
- `buyer_complexity_score ≤ 5`
- Identified contact

**Expected Yield:**
- 1C high-fit: ~480-640 logos
- 2A high-fit: ~100-135 logos

## Wave 2: Core Mid-Market (~5,000 contacts)

**Subtiers:** 1A (10-30 PCPs) + 1C (remaining)

**Why Second:**
- Proven messaging from Wave 1
- Sweet spot PCP size (10-30)
- Still relatively short sales cycles

**Focus:**
- Mid-market provider groups
- Groups with ambient AI already deployed (integration fit)
- Groups with BHI/PCMH recognition

## Wave 3: Broader Reach (~8,000 contacts)

**Subtiers:** 1B + 2C + smaller 1A (5-9 PCPs)

**Why Third:**
- Refined from Wave 1-2 learnings
- Smaller groups with simpler decision-making
- Care management companies for BH workflow fit

**Adjustments:**
- Messaging optimized from previous waves
- Objection handling playbook tested
- Proven conversion patterns

## Parallel: Enterprise (~1,740 contacts)

**Subtiers:** 3A + 3B + 3C

**Motion:** Enterprise multi-threaded, warm intro preferred

**Characteristics:**
- Lower volume, higher touch
- Separate domain (premium)
- Multi-persona outreach (4 contacts per account)
- DO NOT lead with C-suite - lead with VP Pop Health / VP Care Transformation

## Targeted: Partnerships (~200 contacts)

**Subtier:** 2B (VBC Enablement)

**Motion:** Partnership conversations, not campaigns

**Characteristics:**
- 1:1 personalized outreach
- Separate domain or personal email
- VP Partnerships / VP Product as entry
- Channel multiplier value (1 Aledade = 1,000+ practices)

## Contact Volume by Outreach Type

| Type | Contacts | Domain Strategy | Velocity |
|------|----------|-----------------|----------|
| High-velocity cold (1A, 1B, 1C, 2A, 2C) | ~21,765 | Multiple domains, warmed | Higher volume |
| Partnership outreach (2B) | ~200 | Separate domain or personal | 1:1 personalized |
| Enterprise multi-thread (3A, 3B, 3C) | ~1,740 | Separate domain, premium | Lower volume |

## Domain & Infrastructure Planning

| Input | Value | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Total unique contacts | ~23,705 | Across all subtiers |
| Cold-outbound contacts | ~21,765 | Subtiers 1A, 1B, 1C, 2A, 2C |
| Sequence length (avg touches) | 4-6 | Per contact |
| Total emails over campaign | ~87,000-130,000 | Contacts × touches |
| Daily sending limit per domain | ~50-80 | Warmed domain, good reputation |
| Domains needed (cold outbound) | 8-15 | Depends on velocity and warmup |
| Warmup period per domain | 2-4 weeks | Before full volume |
| Separate domain for partnerships | 1 | 2B only |
| Separate domain for enterprise | 1-2 | 3A, 3B, 3C |

## Alignment with 30-60-90 Cadence

| Day Range | Execution | Wave Alignment |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| Day 1-30 | Build/test/warm up + dial PCPs | Wave 1 launch |
| Day 30-60 | Measure results, signals | Wave 2 ramp, Wave 1 analysis |
| Day 90+ | Results optimize, expand | Wave 3 launch, refine all |
