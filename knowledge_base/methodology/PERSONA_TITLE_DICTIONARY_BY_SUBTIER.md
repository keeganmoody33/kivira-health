# Persona-Title Dictionary by Subtier

Maps five demo-bookable buying-committee roles to Clay/LinkedIn-searchable job titles by subtier. Every persona listed here is either a decision-maker or someone you could realistically book a demo with.

[VERIFIED: Title patterns derived from job postings, LinkedIn profile analysis, industry benchmarking]

---

## The Five Demo-Bookable Roles

For every account, target these five people:

1. **Operational Owner** — Feels the pain daily; will run the pilot. First outreach target.
2. **Clinical Champion** — Believes the clinical story and can defend it to the committee.
3. **Economic Buyer** — Owns budget or signs the contract.
4. **Technical Gatekeeper** — Can block on EHR, integration, privacy, security, workflow.
5. **BH / Quality Influencer** — Owns behavioral health workflow, quality program, or performance metrics that Kivira directly impacts. Demo-bookable because they need to see the product in action.

**Outreach sequence:** Operator first, Clinical champion second, Economic buyer once pain is validated, Technical gatekeeper in parallel, BH/Quality influencer looped in during demo stage.
