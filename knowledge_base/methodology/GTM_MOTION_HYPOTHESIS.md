# GTM motion hypothesis

[INFERRED: from MARKETANALYSIS.md + public positioning.] The plausible motion is **B2B** into **primary care organizations** with a **land-and-expand** pattern: prove EHR workflow fit, clinician trust, and implementation services, then broaden within the health system.

Success constraints include **integration depth**, **evidence posture** ([[COMPETITIVE_EVIDENCE_GAP]]), and **change management** for CDS adoption.

## Key Points

- Champion map likely spans **CMIO/CIO**, **behavioral health leadership**, and **primary care operations**.
- Every outbound asset should pass the **CDS framing** check ([[CDS_NOT_DIAGNOSIS_FRAMING]]).

## Evidence

> "Adoption of EMR-integrated clinical decision support is repeatedly shown to be constrained by workflow, usability, organizational approval, and implementation resources."
