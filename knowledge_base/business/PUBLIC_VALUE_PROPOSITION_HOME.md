# Public value proposition (marketing home)

The homepage positions Kivira as integrating **evidence-based screening**, **digital phenotyping**, and **AI analytics** (DSM-5 alignment referenced) with **EHR-native workflow** and **accuracy** motifs (including animated statistics). [VERIFIED: raw-context/kivira-public/www-home.md]

**GTM discipline:** Marketing copy uses strong “diagnosis/accuracy” language; **legal** surfaces describe **clinical decision support** and clinician responsibility. Always cross-check outbound copy against [[CDS_NOT_DIAGNOSIS_FRAMING]] and approved claims matrix.

## Key Points

- Demo UI on site lists named instruments (PHQ-9, GAD-7, C-SSRS) and states DSM-style checklists are internal variants—use as nuance for clinical audiences.
- “Time-discounted accuracy” chart on site is a **marketing proof artifact**, not a substitute for peer-reviewed evidence.

## Evidence

> "Integrates evidence-based screening, digital phenotyping, and AI analytics to deliver trusted, precise, and seamless mental health diagnosis."
