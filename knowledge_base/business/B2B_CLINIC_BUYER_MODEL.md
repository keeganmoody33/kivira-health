# B2B clinic buyer model

Patient app terms state that **Kivira sells services to clinics/hospitals** and that the **App is free to patients** while the **Clinic pays Kivira**. [VERIFIED: raw-context/kivira-public/legal-patient-app-terms.md — Fees section.]

No public price sheet was identified in the research synthesis; treat packaging (per site, per clinician, enterprise) as **unknown without internal validation**.

## Key Points

- Economic buyer is the organization; end users include patients (intake) and clinicians (workflow).
- Procurement likely involves IT/EHR, compliance, and clinical leadership—align discovery to [[DISCOVERY_QUESTIONS_PRIMARY_CARE_BUYER]].

## Evidence

> "The App is provided free to patients. Your Clinic pays Kivira for the service."
