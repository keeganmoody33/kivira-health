## Subtier Definitions

### 1A: Mid-Market Provider Groups
- Multi-site, primary-care-led or mixed PCP + specialty
- Independent (not health-system-employed)
- **Floor:** ≥5 PCPs, ≥2 locations
- **Validated count:** 2,350 (from 3,851 after 1C upgrade)

### 1B: Independent PCP Groups
- Independent PCP practice (family, internal, general)
- **Floor:** 3-4 PCPs
- Identifiable decision-maker
- **Validated count:** 1,740 (from 2,338 after 1C upgrade)

### 1C: VBC Provider Groups
- Active participation in MSSP, ACO REACH, PC Flex, or other VBC contract
- PCP presence required
- **Estimated count:** ~3,200 (includes upgrades from 1A/1B)

## Wave 1 Priority

Wave 1 targets logos with:
- `pilot_feasibility_score ≥ 7`
- `buyer_complexity_score ≤ 5`
- Identified contact

**Expected Wave 1 yield from Tier 1:**
- 1C: 15-20% → 480-640 logos
- 1A: 10-12% → 235-282 logos
- 1B: 8-10% → 139-174 logos
- **Total:** ~850-1,100 logos

## Key Insight: VBC Upgrade Logic

Groups in 1A or 1B that ALSO appear as MSSP participants get **upgraded to 1C primary** because:
- VBC participation signals higher buyer intent
- CoCM readiness is higher
- Higher defensible ACV ($90K vs $75K/$40K)
