# Embedded Distribution via VBC Enablers

[INFERRED: Josh Pappas call 2026-04-06 - suggested channel, not yet validated]

VBC enablement companies (Tier 2B in [[GTM_TIER_ARCHITECTURE_9_SUBTIERS]]) represent a potential **embedded distribution channel** rather than direct end-customers.

## The Opportunity

VBC enablement companies help provider groups succeed in value-based care through:
- Analytics and care orchestration
- Risk adjustment and quality ops
- Navigation and patient engagement
- Platform services

These companies have existing relationships with the same provider groups Kivira targets. If Kivira can embed into their platform or workflow, it creates leverage.

## Outreach Approach

**Channel:** LinkedIn video messages (not email)

**Target Personas:**
- VP of Partnerships
- VP of Product
- VP of Clinical Strategy

**Message Frame:**
- Not "buy Kivira" but "embed Kivira"
- Position as capability that enhances their platform
- Focus on mental health identification as gap in their current offering

## Strategic Considerations

### Why This Might Work
- VBC enablers are motivated by anything that improves quality scores and reduces cost
- Mental health identification is often a gap in their platform
- They have existing distribution to providers Kivira wants to reach

### Why This Might Not Work
- Long partnership cycles (6-12 months)
- Revenue share vs. direct sale economics
- Dependency on partner's roadmap and priorities

## Status

**Emergent** - This is a suggested channel from Josh Pappas, not a validated playbook. Test with 10-20 LinkedIn videos before committing resources.
