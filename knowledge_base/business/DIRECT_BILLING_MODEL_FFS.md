# Direct Billing Model (Fee-for-Service)

The Direct Billing Model is the "Day 1 cash flow" play for Kivira. It positions Kivira as a revenue generator for independent clinics and IDNs through automated documentation that enables reliable billing of behavioral health integration codes.

This is the "self-funding" model that demonstrates immediate ROI before longer-term risk adjustment or cost avoidance benefits materialize.

[INFERRED: Gemini directional analysis - verify before external use]

## Key Points

- **Target buyer:** Independent Clinics, IDNs, Primary Care Practices
- **Value driver:** Immediate incremental revenue from BHI billing codes
- **Mechanism:** Kivira automates documentation required to bill screening and care management codes
- **Timeline:** Day 1 value proposition - "pays for itself"

## Relevant CPT Codes

Key codes that Kivira documentation supports (verify current rates):
- **96127** - Brief Emotional/Behavioral Assessment
- **99484** - General Behavioral Health Integration (BHI) monthly
- **G0444** - Annual Depression Screening

## The ROI Logic

The directional formula:

> (Monthly Patient Volume) x (Screening Rate %) x (Per-visit billing value) = Monthly Gross Revenue

For clinics already seeing patients, Kivira turns mental health screening from a "time sink" into a documented, billable service.

## Why This Wins Day 1

This model has the shortest sales cycle because:
1. No risk adjustment complexity to explain
2. No multi-year cost avoidance projections needed
3. Clinic can see incremental revenue in first billing cycle
4. Aligns with Provider/PCP segment as Day 1 focus
