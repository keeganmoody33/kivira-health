# Primary care wedge (ICP)

Kivira’s marketing and legal surfaces consistently emphasize **primary care** as the clinical context: PCPs face rising mental health volume and need workflow-native support. [INFERRED: positioning from www-home + ToS framing of clinic-paid, EHR-integrated screening.]

This node is the **anchor ICP hypothesis** for GTM: sell to clinics/health systems whose PCPs deliver or coordinate behavioral health care.

**Hypothesis Status:** Validated by Josh Pappas call (2026-04-06). Operational owner focus confirmed. Treat messaging as hypothesis until validated by outreach data ([[MESSAGING_HYPOTHESIS_DISCIPLINE]]).

## Key Points

- Public copy positions PCPs as “frontline” for mental health; product promise is precision + EHR fit, not a parallel siloed app for clinicians only.
- [VERIFIED: raw-context/kivira-public/www-home.md] Hero and body stress primary care and EHR workflow integration language.
- Pair outbound messaging with [[CDS_NOT_DIAGNOSIS_FRAMING]] so clinical stakeholders hear decision support, not autonomous diagnosis.
