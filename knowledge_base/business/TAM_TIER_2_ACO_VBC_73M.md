## 2B: VBC Enablement Companies

**Definition:** Company selling analytics, risk adjustment, care orchestration, or platform services to VBC provider groups. Buyer is VP Partnerships or VP Product.

**Estimated Count:** 100 (from industry landscape mapping)

### Motion: Partnership/Embed

These are NOT direct end-customers. The play is:
- Kivira embeds mental health CDS into their platform
- They distribute to their provider network
- Revenue model: licensing fee or per-provider-per-month

**Channel multiplier value:** A single Aledade partnership reaches 1,000+ practices.

### Key Players

Provider Enablement: Aledade, Pearl Health, Vytalize, Wellvana, Privia, agilon, Astrana
Data & Analytics: Arcadia, Innovaccer, Clarify Health, Lightbeam, Persivia
Point-of-Care: Vim, Navina, Holon Solutions
Quality/Risk: Episource, Vatica Health, Apixio

## 2C: Care Management Companies

**Definition:** Organization delivering care coordination, chronic care management, or BHI services. Employs care managers who interact with PCP workflows.

**Estimated Count:** 180 (from industry landscape)

### Key Players

Outsourced: Matrix Medical, Signify Health, Landmark Health, Carelon
Tech-Enabled: Cityblock Health, Pair Team, Wellbe Health
BH-Specific: NeuroFlow, Quartet Health, Lucet
