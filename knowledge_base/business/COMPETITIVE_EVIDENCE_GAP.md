# Competitive evidence gap

The deep research synthesis argues that the **largest diligence gap** is **Kivira-specific clinical validation** (peer-reviewed outcomes, external benchmarks, bias auditing, real-world utility). Until published and reviewed, **website performance visuals** should be handled as **marketing claims** only.

## Key Points

- Enterprise buyers will ask for evidence; prepare an **evidence packet** tiering: public instruments → internal validation status → roadmap.
- This pattern **constrains** how aggressively sales can cite accuracy numbers from the homepage ([[PUBLIC_VALUE_PROPOSITION_HOME]]).
