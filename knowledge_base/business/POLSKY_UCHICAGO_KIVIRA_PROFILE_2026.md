## Summary (atomic claims as stated in the article)

- **Founder:** Maria T. Carmona, MBA ’25 (Booth); origin story tied to family misdiagnosis and a pivot from an early consumer concept to **clinician-first B2B**. [VERIFIED: Polsky article.]
- **Discovery:** Interviews including **300+ primary care providers**; UChicago Medicine, advisors, legal; emphasis on trust, workflow, adoption. [VERIFIED: Polsky article.]
- **Program / prize:** Polsky **Global New Venture Challenge**; quote that “GNVC was where Kivira was really born”; **first place, 18th Annual GNVC, $50,000**. [VERIFIED: Polsky article + linked GNVC results page in archive.]
- **Product story (press):** Integration into existing systems; structured assessment triggered from **primary care**; “validated methodologies” and **ML on clinical data**; **diagnostic report** in existing workflow. [VERIFIED: Polsky article.]
- **Milestones (press):** **Medication recommendation model** development completed (in-house team); expansion of patients/providers; **$1.8M pre-seed led by Wellstar** (11 hospitals, 450+ outpatient sites). [VERIFIED: Polsky article.]
- **Vision (press):** “Diagnostic infrastructure” beyond primary care (hospitals, practices, universities, EAPs, veterans, government). [VERIFIED: Polsky article.]
