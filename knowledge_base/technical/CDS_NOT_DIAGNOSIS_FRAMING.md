# CDS, not autonomous diagnosis (framing)

Patient terms state that AI-assisted outputs **support human decision-making** and **do not replace clinical judgment**; the clinician remains responsible for diagnosis and treatment. Privacy policy reiterates human review for recommendations in AI-assisted features. [VERIFIED: legal-patient-app-terms.md §5; legal-privacy-policy.md §5.]

## Key Points

- External messaging must not imply standalone diagnosis or replacement of the treating clinician.
- Use this node as the **lint rule** when editing website copy, decks, and outbound sequences ([[PUBLIC_VALUE_PROPOSITION_HOME]]).

## Evidence
