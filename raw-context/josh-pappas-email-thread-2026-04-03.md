### 1. The "Revenue Accuracy" Model (Risk Adjustment)

Target: Medicare Advantage Plans & ACOs

CMS uses Hierarchical Condition Categories (HCCs) to determine payments. If a PCP misses a diagnosis or under-codes severity, the plan loses thousands per member.

**Variables:**
- HCC 243 (Major Depressive Disorder, Severe) - significant PMPM value
- HCC 241 (Bipolar Disorder) - significant PMPM value
- Kivira Detection Lift potential

**ROI Formula:**
> (Total Members) x (Est. missed diagnosis %) x (Avg. HCC Value) = Total Found Revenue

### 2. The "Medical Spend" Model (ER Avoidance)

Target: Full-Risk VBC Groups & Health Systems

**Variables:**
- Avg. Cost of Psychiatric ER Visit - significant
- Avg. Cost of Mental Health Boarding - significant per day
- Kivira potential reduction in admissions

**ROI Formula:**
> (Annual Psych ER Visits) x (Reduction %) x (Avg. Cost) = Direct Cost Savings

### 3. The "Direct Billing" Model (Immediate Fee-for-Service)

Target: Independent Clinics & IDNs

**Key CPT Codes:**
- 96127 - Brief Emotional/Behavioral Assessment
- 99484 - General Behavioral Health Integration (BHI)
- G0444 - Annual Depression Screening

**ROI Formula:**
> (Monthly Patient Volume) x (Screening Rate %) x (Per-visit value) = Monthly Gross Revenue
