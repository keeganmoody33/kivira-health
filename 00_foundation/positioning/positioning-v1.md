# Kivira positioning (v1, public-grounded)

**Layer 2 doc —** composes atomic nodes; do not duplicate long explanations here.

## Who we serve

- **Primary economic buyer:** Clinics and health systems delivering **primary care–centric** behavioral health ([[PRIMARY_CARE_WEDGE_ICP]], [[B2B_CLINIC_BUYER_MODEL]]).
- **Users:** Patients (structured intake/screening on the free patient app) and clinicians receiving **CDS-oriented** outputs inside existing workflows ([[CDS_NOT_DIAGNOSIS_FRAMING]]).

## What we do (defensible phrasing)

- Combine **evidence-based instruments** (public reference list: [[CLINICAL_INSTRUMENTS_SURFACE]]) with **digital phenotyping** and **analytics** described on the marketing site as aligned to DSM-5-style framing—**always** paired with clinician judgment.
- **Integrate** via **SMART on FHIR / HL7-class** patterns; Epic MyChart invitation flow is documented publicly ([[EHR_INTEGRATION_SMART_ON_FHIR]]).

## What we are not (guardrails)

- **Not** emergency services or continuous monitoring ([[PATIENT_APP_SAFETY_ALERTS]]).
- **Not** a substitute for clinician diagnosis/treatment decisions ([[CDS_NOT_DIAGNOSIS_FRAMING]]).
- **Not** a peer-reviewed outcomes engine **until** Kivira-specific studies ship ([[COMPETITIVE_EVIDENCE_GAP]]).

## Proof strategy

- **Legitimacy:** Team + medical board + investor logos as **approved** by marketing/legal ([[FUNDING_AND_BACKING_SIGNALS]]).
- **Clinical credibility:** Instruments + citations page + honest separation of third-party vs product validation ([[EVIDENCE_DISCIPLINE_RESEARCH_SYNTHESIS]]).
- **Enterprise trust:** HIPAA BAA posture and AI disclosures ([[PRIVACY_AND_HIPAA_ROLE]]).
