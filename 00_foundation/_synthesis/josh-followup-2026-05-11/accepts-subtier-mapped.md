## What this tells us about Wave 1 list quality

**10 in-scope out of 58 = 17% accept-to-fit rate.** The list-side filters did some work — the 1C and 2A campaigns each produced a few high-confidence accepts that match the intended persona. The bigger drag is the **Baseline 9-Subtier campaign**, which pulled disproportionately from generic "healthcare growth" LinkedIn slices and dragged in most of the GTM-tooling noise.

**Implication for Wave 2:** tighten the Baseline-equivalent list to exclude obvious vendor/tool-side titles ("Founder + small AI startup," "GTM Engineer," "Sales Rep," "Lead Gen") before they hit outreach. Filter rules already exist in the universal-exclusions section of our subtier framework — they just weren't tight enough on titles.
